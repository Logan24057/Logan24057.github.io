Projects

I am currently working on a classroom project called Onlypogs. It is a digital library of pogs for the students to look at and use. Pogs are a token-like form of classroom currency that is used to motivate students to work harder and better. Students are also awarded pogs for doing extra work and participating in school events or clubs, as well as taking opportunities outside of school to further their careers.
